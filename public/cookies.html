This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit this website.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help sites work efficiently and provide reporting information.

Types of cookies we use

CategoryPurpose
Strictly necessaryRequired for the site to function (routing, security, remembering your cookie choice).
PreferencesRemember settings and quiz progress locally in your browser.
AnalyticsHelp us understand how visitors interact with the site (may include Google Analytics).
AdvertisingUsed, where enabled, to measure and deliver relevant ads (e.g., Google Ads). Loaded only with consent where required.

Third-party cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services such as Google. Learn more at policies.google.com/technologies/cookies.

Your choices

  • Use the cookie banner on the homepage to accept or decline non-essential cookies.
  • Manage cookies in your browser settings.
  • Opt out of personalized Google ads at adssettings.google.com.

Changes
